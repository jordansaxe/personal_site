Who I am

A design-focused Product Manager/Strategist with over 4 years of industry experience working with web and mobile applications.

Since I was a little kid, I spent all of my free time online. I knew I wanted to take that passion and turn it into a career in the online space. I completed by BA in Business Technology Management honing my Business and Technology skills, while also working for a wide variety of companies. Since then, I have refined my focus to help small startups to large businesses complete their goals within their time-frames.

Over the years I have continued to invest in furthering my knowledge on all things web and mobile. I have attended HackerYou's Intro to Web Design and Intro to Ruby on Rails which allow me to easily create and customize a full-suite of dynamic web applications. As well this gives me the knowledge to speak with design driven teams with confidence in the proposed solution.
